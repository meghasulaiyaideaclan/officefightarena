Champions of the Realms

Strike, sweep, and hurl relics at rival champions summoned to the Grand Convergence. Journey through the Sacred Grove, Sky Sanctum, Hall of the First Trial, Mystic Garden and Elderwood Wilds to gather Blessings and find the hidden Relic of Ascension. Up to 7 champions per trial.

How to Play

🎯 The Grand Convergence

Once every hundred years, champions from every realm are summoned to compete in the Sacred Trials. Strike, sweep, and hurl relics to defeat rival champions. You have 3 lives — survive until the trial ends, or be the last champion standing.

🎮 Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys / Joystick — move
  • F — strike
  • G — sweep
  • E — take up / hurl a relic, or grapple / hurl a champion

🥊 Combat

Strikes are fast and light, sweeps hit harder but are slower. Landing a hit knocks the target back. Dropping to 0 HP costs a life and respawns you with a few seconds of invulnerability.

🍕 Blessings

Blessing of Swiftness, Spirit Herb, Sunfruit Nectar, Blessing of Power and Ancient Feast are scattered across the realms — walk over one to heal. Blessing of Swiftness also grants a speed boost and Blessing of Power a damage boost for a few seconds.

📦 Relics & grappling champions

Throwing Shards, Rune Tablets and Ironwood Boughs can be taken up (E) and hurled (E again) at other champions for extra damage. Watch for the rare Sundering Boulder — a direct hit defeats a champion instantly. You can also grapple a nearby rival instead of a relic — carry them and hurl them into someone else!

🌍 Explore the realms

Ley Portals connect the Sacred Grove → Sky Sanctum → Hall of the First Trial → Mystic Garden Terrace → Elderwood Wilds. Step onto a portal's arrow to travel between realms — each one has its own relics, Blessings, and hiding spots.

🏆 Honor

The trial's winner is whoever has earned the most Honor - not whoever survives! Honor is completely separate from lives: claim the hidden Relic of Ascension (+1000), complete a Sacred Trial (+300), grapple and hurl a champion (+100), or gather a Blessing (+20). Check the Hall of Honor to see who's leading.

👑 The Ascendant

A hidden Relic of Ascension spawns somewhere across the realms. Whoever has the most Honor wears a crown above their head as the current "Ascendant" - the same champion who'll be named Protector of the Realms if the Honor holds.

📋 Sacred Trials

Every so often, a Sacred Trial is announced to every champion (defeat rivals, ascend to the Sky Sanctum, endure unscathed, and more). First champion to complete it earns bonus Honor.

👹 The Ancient Guardian

Periodically the Ancient Guardian appears with a warning - every champion sees it, wherever they are. When it delivers its Judgment, anyone not sheltered by a Wardstone (a pillar, bench, fountain, or tree) takes a heavy hit. Find shelter fast!
