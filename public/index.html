Office Fight Arena

Punch, kick, and hurl staplers at your coworkers. Grab coffee, tea, lemonade, pizza and burgers around the office to heal up. Take the staircase to the rooftop lounge and find the hidden crown to become King of the Clan. Up to 7 players per room.

How to Play

🎯 Objective

Punch, kick, and throw office items to knock out other players. You have 3 lives — survive until time runs out, or be the last one standing.

🎮 Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys / Joystick — move
  • F — punch
  • G — kick
  • E — pick up / throw an item, or grab / throw a player

🥊 Combat

Punches are fast and light, kicks hit harder but are slower. Landing a hit knocks the target back. Dropping to 0 HP costs a life and respawns you with a few seconds of invulnerability.

🍕 Power-ups

Coffee, tea, lemonade, pizza and burgers are scattered around the office — walk over one to heal. Coffee also grants a speed boost and pizza a damage boost for a few seconds.

📦 Office items & grabbing players

Staplers, keyboards and chairs can be picked up (E) and thrown (E again) at other players for extra damage. You can also grab a nearby opponent instead of an item — carry them and throw them into someone else!

🏢 The rooftop

Step onto the ▲ staircase to head up to the rooftop lounge, or the ▼ staircase to come back down to the main office floor.

👑 King of the Clan

A hidden golden crown spawns somewhere in the office or upstairs. Whoever has collected the most crowns wears one above their head and is crowned King of the Clan.

📋 Tasks

Every so often, a task is announced to the whole match (land KOs, reach the rooftop, survive without damage, and more). First player to complete it earns bonus crown score.
